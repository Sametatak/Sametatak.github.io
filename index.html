About Me

I am a senior Control and Automation Engineering student at Istanbul Technical University (ITU), graduating in 2026. My primary interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, autonomous robotics, and ensemble learning methods.

I served as a Machine Learning Researcher at the University of Kassel, Germany, where I focused on automated hyperparameter tuning for ensemble regression models and contributed to a research paper on the topic.

I also have hands-on experience as an Autonomous Robot Developer at Machine Arge. In this role, I developed autonomous algorithms using ROS, implemented path planning and obstacle avoidance, and worked with embedded systems (STM32) and various sensors (Intel RealSense, GPS-IMU) to achieve robust real-time performance.
